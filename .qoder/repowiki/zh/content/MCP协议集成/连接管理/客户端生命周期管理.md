Disconnected --> |否| Monitor
AutoReconnect --> ReconnectSuccess{重连成功?}
ReconnectSuccess --> |是| Connected
ReconnectSuccess --> |否| MarkFailed[标记为失败]
MarkFailed --> Failed
ReturnCache --> Connected

flowchart TD
